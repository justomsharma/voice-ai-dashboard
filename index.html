The three takeaways

1. The default stack nobody disputes: LiveKit + Deepgram Nova-3 + GPT-4o/Gemini Flash + ElevenLabs or Cartesia.

2. Speech-to-speech is the future, not the present: 3–6× more expensive and harder to audit. Consumer apps first, enterprise later.

3. "Premium" ≠ best: Scribe v2 now beats Nova-3 on WER. Inworld TTS beats ElevenLabs on preference. Test on your own audio.

Five stacks that deliver ~90% of premium quality at 30–55% of premium cost. Each has documented production adopters.

V1 — The defensible default ⭐
LiveKit + Deepgram Nova-3 + Gemini 2.5 Flash + Cartesia Sonic 2
~50% savings

Gemini Flash is ~8× cheaper than GPT-4o at similar TTFT. Cartesia Sonic matches ElevenLabs latency at ~40% of per-char price. Used by Thoughtly, 11x (Julian), countless YC voice agents.

Cost: $0.10–$0.14/min · Verdict: Adopt this unless you have a specific reason not to.
V2 — The latency pick
Groq Whisper Turbo + Groq Llama 3.3 70B + Deepgram Aura-2 + Pipecat
~55% savings

Wins when sub-400ms TTFT is non-negotiable. Groq's LPU delivers the most consistent tail latency in the market (276–315 tok/s). Aura-2 is ~40% cheaper than ElevenLabs Flash.

Cost: $0.08–$0.12/min · Watch out: Groq capacity tiers have caused outages (Llama 4 Maverick, Nov 2025).
V3 — The cheapest cascade
AssemblyAI Universal-Streaming + GPT-4o-mini + Rime Mist + Pipecat
~60% savings

Cheapest managed-grade cascade at acceptable quality. Universal-Streaming emits words in 300ms — beats Nova-3 on median latency. Rime Mist at ~$0.005/min is the cheapest production TTS. Used in Retell/Synthflow backends.

Cost: $0.07–$0.10/min
V4 — The MLOps pick
NVIDIA Parakeet TDT v3 + Cerebras Llama 3.3 70B + Kokoro 82M + LiveKit OSS
~75–85% savings

For teams that can run GPU ops. Kokoro at ~$0.65/M chars is ~15× cheaper than ElevenLabs. Parakeet hits RTFx 2000+ on one GPU. Cerebrium's demo proved 500ms voice-to-voice on exactly this class.

Cost: $0.03–$0.06/min at volume · Needs: Full MLOps team.
V5 — The absolute latency pick
LiveKit + Deepgram Flux + Gemini 2.5 Flash-Lite + Cartesia Sonic 3
~55% savings

Flux bundles ASR with built-in semantic turn detection — no separate VAD tuning. Gemini Flash-Lite holds the lowest AA TTFT at 0.39s. Sonic 3 adds 40+ languages. Best for sub-700ms end-to-end.

Cost: $0.08–$0.12/min
